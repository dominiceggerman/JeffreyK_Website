About Jeffrey Kenney

I am an observational astronomer who investigates the structure and evolution of galaxies. I am interested in the things that promote and stop star formation so I study the interstellar medium, particularly molecular gas. A major focus of my work has been the environmental effects on galaxies in clusters, especially ram pressure stripping. I like nearby galaxies that can be studied with good resolution and sensitivity so that you can see what is actually happening. Many of my projects have studied the galaxies in the nearby Virgo cluster.
